MAKEUP TIPS

  • Prep Your Skin
    Cleanse your face, moisturize, and apply sunscreen. Optionally, use a primer to smooth your skin and enhance makeup longevity.

  • Base
    Use a lightweight foundation, BB cream, or tinted moisturizer for an even tone. Apply concealer to cover under-eye circles, blemishes, or redness. Lightly set oily areas with translucent powder.

  • Brows
    Brush your brows upward with a spoolie. Fill in sparse areas with a pencil or powder, mimicking natural hair strokes. Set with clear or tinted brow gel for a polished look.

  • Eyes
    Apply a neutral eyeshadow to your lids, using a slightly darker shade in the crease for soft definition. Tightline your upper lash line with a brown or black eyeliner. Apply one or two coats of mascara on your upper lashes; keep the lower lashes light.

  • Cheeks
    Use a cream or powder blush in natural shades (pink, peach, or mauve). Blend on the apples of your cheeks and toward your temples. Optionally, dust bronzer lightly on your cheekbones and forehead for warmth. Add a subtle highlighter to the high points of your face for a soft glow.

  • Lips
    Prep with a lip balm.Choose a tinted balm, gloss, or nude lipstick that matches your natural lip color.

  • Set and Blend
    Use a setting spray to lock in the look. Double-check for seamless blending and adjust as needed.

    • This creates a polished, natural appearance perfect for everyday wear!
