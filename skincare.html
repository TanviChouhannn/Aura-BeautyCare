SKINCARE TIPS

  • Cleansing:Wash your face twice daily (morning and evening) with a gentle cleanser suited to your skin type (oily, dry, combination, or sensitive). Avoid harsh soaps that can strip your skin of natural oils.


  • Exfoliation:Exfoliate 1â€“3 times a week to remove dead skin cells and promote skin renewal. Use gentle scrubs or chemical exfoliants like AHAs (glycolic or lactic acid) or BHAs (salicylic acid).


  • Moisturizing:Always moisturize after cleansing, even if your skin is oily. Look for lightweight, non-comedogenic options if needed. Use richer creams for dry skin or in colder weather.


  • Sun Protection:Apply sunscreen (SPF 30 or higher) daily, even on cloudy days or indoors (UV rays can penetrate windows). Reapply every 2 hours if outdoors.
