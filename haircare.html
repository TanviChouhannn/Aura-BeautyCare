HAIRCARE TIPS

  • Know Your Hair Type: Understand if your hair is straight, wavy, curly, or coily, and whether it is fine, thick, or porous. This helps tailor your routine.

  • Choose the Right Products: Use shampoos, conditioners, and treatments designed for your hair type (e.g., moisturizing for dry hair, clarifying for oily hair, sulfate-free for sensitive scalps).

  • Avoid Overwashing: Washing too often can strip natural oils. Aim for 2 times a week or as needed.

  • Use Lukewarm Water: Hot water can dry out your hair and scalp; opt for lukewarm or cool water for rinsing.
